Sponsors

Open Web Docs offers tiered sponsorship levels in order to support participation from a diverse group of organizations based on size, financials, and resource bandwidth. Our Membership page outlines the sponsorship levels available to organizations.

On our open collective page or on GitHub you can make one-time donations or set up monthly or yearly recurring contributions.

Thank you so much for supporting OWD! ❤️
